# 1. Non-Standard / Custom Open Licenses (These are open, but not explicitly on the pre-approved list. PCounsel must verify them).

**Open Government Licence v3.0** — While legally interoperable with CC-BY 4.0, it is technically a UK OGL license, which is not on the pre-approved list.
**Government Open Data License - India** — Custom government open license.
**Government of Japan Standard Terms of Use** — Custom government open license.
**CBS Custom Open License** — Custom open license.
**Creative Commons Attribution-NoDerivatives 3.0 Unported** — This is CC-BY-ND, which is more restrictive than standard CC-BY and is not on the list.


# 2. Ambiguous "Open" Licenses (The document states Public Domain is allowed "as long as no further restrictions apply. If you have questions, please contact your pcounsel".)

**Free Use License** — "Free Use" is legally ambiguous. PCounsel must review the specific dataset terms to see if it truly acts as Public Domain.
**Open Access** — Open Access is an umbrella term, not a specific license. PCounsel needs to review the exact URL to confirm it's CC-BY equivalent.
